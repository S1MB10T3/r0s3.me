<Intro>
  ![Flower pot at A Place From Home](/media/flowerpot/1.webp)

  A large modular flower pot. Individual blocks working together to support life.
  A home for me isn't complete without veggies and plants around. Having a large
  pot that I can shift and resize provides a sense of comfort that I can always
  have a place to grow something.

  _Photo above is a picture taken at A Place From Home art show at St Dymphna's,
  a bar in the East Village NYC._

  Constructed out of wood, steel, and canvas.
</Intro>
