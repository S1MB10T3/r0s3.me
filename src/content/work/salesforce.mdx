  ### Style

  ### Layout

  #### Cards

  Cards were chosen to keep consistency with Salesforce's own platform design.
  Additionally, minor, easy-to-develop animations were added to Cards for a
  more engaging user experience.

  <video autoPlay playsInline loop muted src="media/salesforce/cards.webm" />

  #### Activities

  Impactful gameplay choices would happen on the Decisions Page, where users face
  game prompts and challenges and choose answers from a preselected list. These
  prompts and challenges all pertain to a fictional company named Allin.

  <video autoPlay playsInline loop muted src="media/salesforce/challenges.webm" />

  In one scenario, the player needs to pitch Allin's services to a potential
  client company. The player is shown a number of potential challenges the
  potential client faces that perhaps Allin can solve. Players can drag and
  drop their top 4 solvable challenges into their answer to complete the task.

  In another scenario, the player "interviews" Allin Employee NPCs by choosing
  one of several predefined question categories, followed by a question from
  a predefined list. The NPC can either respond with helpful insight to the
  question, point the player to the right person to ask, or provide a
  non-response.

  <video autoPlay playsInline loop muted src="media/salesforce/chat.webm" />

  #### Metrics

  Teams are able to keep track of their Key Performance Indicators on the KPI
  page. This page allows a player to see how well they are doing and get feedback
  on prior decisions through intuitive charts. They're also able to check how
  much total knowledge they've unlocked through the game through the Knowledge
  Page.
