<Intro>
  How do we prepare managers for the new world of hybrid work teams? This was
  my first project while working at Forio for Facebook (now Meta) when I was
  brought in mid 2021.
</Intro>

<Section title="early draft">
  Made a quick mock up of a potential simulation design for Facebook
  Management Team. Since we were still figuring out the right approach to
  what learning points we are trying to hit. Hybrid work is a new world and
  there's no one set of answers to teach Managers to run their teams when
  they're split across buildings, homes, and timezones.

  <div className="page-media-row">
    ![Early dashboard mockup](media/facebook-mdt/old-dashboard.png)

    ![Early schedule mockup](media/facebook-mdt/old-schedule.png)
  </div>

  Started off building out screens that are useful for possible management
  scenarios like a Dashboard to view your team, Calendar for planning
  meetings, and a Video Chat screen for meetings.

  ![Early NPC detail view](media/facebook-mdt/old-npc.png)

  A detailed view of the player NPC team members. Providing a current
  emotional state and some KPI details.
</Section>

<Section title="final design">
  An issue with the early prototype was a lack of gameplay direction. The
  notification bar provides a clear list of things that the player has to do
  as well as a historical list of actions taken throughout the simulation.

  <div className="page-media-row">
    ![Dashboard world map](media/facebook-mdt/map.png)

    ![Pulse Results KPIs](media/facebook-mdt/kpis.png)
  </div>

  In addition the dashboard has been changed to provide a map of where the
  players team members are located across the globe, a list displaying all
  the NPC's Pulse Results (a metric used internally at Meta), and an org
  chart.

  The character bio became a popup modal making it easier to access and leave
  across the simulation on different pages.

  ![NPC bio modal](media/facebook-mdt/npc.png)

  The chat system has been pulled from prior Facebook simulations. By
  clicking on message segments players can form a message which they can
  send. Based on the segments chosen the model will give a positive or
  negative response.

  <div className="page-media-row">
    ![Segment chat, composing](media/facebook-mdt/chat1.png)

    ![Segment chat, response](media/facebook-mdt/chat2.png)
  </div>

  1 on 1 video calls displays the NPC's Pulse Results and changes in real
  time based on the users response using the same chat system in the group
  chat. While group video calls requires the player to mark flags of
  instances negative traits and interactions during the meeting.

  <div className="page-media-row">
    ![1 on 1 video call](media/facebook-mdt/vc1.png)

    ![Group video call](media/facebook-mdt/vc2.png)
  </div>
</Section>
